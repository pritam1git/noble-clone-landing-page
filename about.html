How we started

It was the perfect Pacific Northwest day: partly sunny when we arrived in downtown Tacoma for a Tex-Mex lunch, and dumping rain by the time we left. It was at this meeting that Joe shared his vision for the builder market with those of us who are now on the Noble Interiors and Design team. This market has plenty of contractors who provide interior finishes for builders, but this – THIS – must be different. Set apart. It is purposeful design and service hinged on a people-centric culture.
